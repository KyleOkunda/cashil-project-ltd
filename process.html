    How We Work

    Our Process

    A proven methodology refined over 18 years, ensuring every project is delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

    Phase 1

    Discovery & Feasibility

    Site assessment, geological surveys, and preliminary cost analysis.

    • Initial client consultation
    • Site inspection and surveys
    • Feasibility study report
    • Budget estimation
    Duration: 2-4 weeks
    Phase 2

    Design & Permits

    Architectural drawings, structural engineering, and regulatory approvals.

    • Detailed architectural plans
    • Structural engineering
    • NCA & County permits
    • Environmental compliance
    Duration: 4-8 weeks
    Phase 3

    Construction Administration

    On-site execution with quality control and progress monitoring.

    • Material procurement
    • Site mobilization
    • Quality assurance
    • Progress reporting
    Duration: Project dependent
    Phase 4

    Handover & Support

    Final inspection, documentation, and post-completion support.

    • Final walkthrough
    • Snag list resolution
    • Documentation handover
    • 12-month warranty
    Duration: 2-4 weeks
