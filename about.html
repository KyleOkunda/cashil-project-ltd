    Our Story

    Bridging Industrial Strength with Design Excellence

    Cashil Projects Limited was founded in 2005 by James Mwangi, a civil engineer with a vision to bring international construction standards to Kenya's rapidly growing infrastructure sector.

    Starting with road construction and civil works, we quickly established a reputation for quality, reliability, and on-time delivery. Our commitment to excellence earned us NCA Class 1 certification in 2012.

    In 2015, we expanded into interior design and fit-outs, recognizing the growing demand for premium spaces that match Kenya's ambitious infrastructure. This unique dual capability allows us to offer end-to-end solutions.
